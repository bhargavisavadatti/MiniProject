About This Tool

What is FinSmart?

A free tool designed to improve your financial awareness and stability

What It Does

FinSmart analyses your income, expenses, savings and emergency fund to calculate a Financial Stability Score and identify your financial personality type.

How It Helps

You get a personalised score, goal tracking with progress bars, a 10-question personality quiz and tailored money management advice — all saved in your browser.

Your Privacy

All your data stays on your device. Nothing is sent to any server. Your profile, scores and goals are saved locally in your browser only.
